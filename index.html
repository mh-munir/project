CyberCaravan

Cyber Hacking Caravan:

The Command Center for Future Tech Heroes – We Hack the Present to Shape the Future!

Embark on a journey of discovery and empowerment with our one-day workshop in Cybersecurity, Linux, and AI.

Why Attend?

Hands-on Learning:

Immerse yourself in the practical aspects of cybersecurity, AI, and Linux.

Inspiring Journeys:

Explore the landscape of tech through interactive workshops.

Future-Ready Skills:

Equip yourself for the evolving world of tech.

Who Should Attend?

  • High school and college students who are ready to delve into tech.
  • Young innovators and tech enthusiasts.
  • Any youth interested in shaping their future or protecting their community from cyberwarfare.

What Will You Gain?

A practical introduction to ethical hacking and cybersecurity.

An understanding of Generative AI and its transformative power.

Practical introduction to Linux and its pervasiveness.

About Us:

Pascal Fenkam, PhD, is an entrepreneur, researcher, software developer, and the founder of CyberCaravan. He's a Red Hat Certified Architect, with 20+ years in IT, working for many Fortune 500 companies.

Pascal teaches at Seneca College and works as a Solutions Architect at Red Hat, an IBM Company.
